About Sofrecom

Sofrecom, subsidiary of the Orange group, is a consulting and engineering firm specialized in telecommunications.
Sofrecom supports the development and the digital transformation of the main actors of the telecommunications sector: operators, governments and international institutions. We advise them in their strategic decisions regarding technologies and growth.
